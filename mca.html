Department of computer application

The Department of Computer Applications was started in the year 1997 initially with the intake of 30 which is now increased to 60. Keeping in mind today’s fast paced world’s need the MCA course is designed to make software professionals in the field of computer applications with a blend of Management techniques keeping emphasis on application with sound theoretical knowledge.
